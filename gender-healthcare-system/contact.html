Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

You can schedule an appointment in several ways: online through our appointment booking system, by calling our office during business hours, or by visiting us in person. For new patients, we recommend completing the registration form online before your first visit to save time.

We accept most major insurance plans. To confirm if your specific plan is covered, please contact our billing department with your insurance details, or check our website's Insurance Information section for a complete list of accepted providers.

Patients can access their medical records through our secure patient portal. If you haven't registered for the portal yet, please contact our office for assistance. For official record requests, please complete the Medical Records Release Form available on our website or at our office.

For your first visit, please bring your government-issued photo ID, insurance card, completed patient forms (available online for pre-filling), a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous healthcare providers.
